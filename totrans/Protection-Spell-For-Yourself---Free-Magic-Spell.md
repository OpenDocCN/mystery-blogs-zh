Protects yourself from negativity,evil and ill intent spells

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Protection Spell For Yourself'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Four White Candles

*   Jug

*   Vinegar

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Four White Candles

*   Jug

*   Vinegar
