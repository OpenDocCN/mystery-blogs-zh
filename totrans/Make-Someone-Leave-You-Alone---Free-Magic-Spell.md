# Make Someone Leave You Alone

  Rated 5/5 Stars

Cast this if you want to make someone leave you.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Make Someone Leave You Alone'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Black candle that hasnt been used

*   Little black paper

*   Pen (if you cant see the letters written in the paper it doesnt matter, just that its written matters)

*   A bowl - doesnt matter which one

*   A object that you can make the candles flame go away ex. a glass

*   Black candle that hasnt been used

*   Little black paper

*   Pen (if you cant see the letters written in the paper it doesnt matter, just that its written matters)

*   A bowl - doesnt matter which one

*   A object that you can make the candles flame go away ex. a glass

 Take the piece of paper and write the persons full name and birth (the person you want away). Light the candle and take the paper and bend it, hold it in your hand and chant 1x while looking at the flame

" This person shall go away
This is my will and I wont regret it.
I am casting a spell on you for my own good.
I don't want you in my life so leave me and so mote it be. "

Take the paper and burn it and then put it in the bowl so you wont hurt yourself and chant 5x

" Darkness ended,
Control is done.
Light has come,
My battle's won "

Then take object ex. glass and blow the candles flame down through the glass. Take the candle outside and bury it.
