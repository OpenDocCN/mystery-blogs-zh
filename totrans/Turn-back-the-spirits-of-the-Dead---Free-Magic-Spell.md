Cast away evil doing spirits.

*   5 Mistletoe berries

*   Black altar cloth

*   small square of altar cloth

*   5 black candles

*   Glass cup

Cover your altar with the black cloth, start your candles burning. Put your carnation pettles in the vodka to soak while they are soaking set your chicken bones in a row and say;

flesh lacking blood

bone lacking flesh

spirit lacking bone

arise and be fed

arise to bone and flesh and blood

this night renewed by mortal food

nevermore to thirst for mine

nor on my living limbs to twine

Strain the vodka from the carnation pettles in the glass, then add the wine and then the bone, mix this well. Take a little of the potion and sprinkle it over the mistletoe berries. remove the bone from the glass and put them in the small black then 2 of the berries bury these in a grave yard. Then take the remaining 3 berries and put them in a sealed jar with the wine and vodka mixture. This jar will keep away any other spirits who wish to bother you.
