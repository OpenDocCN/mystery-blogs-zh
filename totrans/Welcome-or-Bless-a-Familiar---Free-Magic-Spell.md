# Welcome or Bless a Familiar

Bast is the goddess of cats, Hecate is the patron of dogs and snakes. Herne is good for almost any wild animal, should you happen to have one for a familiar. If your animal will sit still for this magickal working, it is nice to have them there for the spell, but you can do it without them. If they are not present, substitute a picture.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Welcome or Bless a Familiar'

*   White candle (or any candle in the shape of your familiar)

*   A statue or picture of Bast, Hecate, or another appropriate animal patron
