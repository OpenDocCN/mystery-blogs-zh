# Good fortune for a friend

A spell to give your friend luck and good fortune.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Good fortune for a friend'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Orange candle

*   A piece of paper 

*   Pen 

*   Lighter 

*   A bracelet or anything you want to give her/him

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Orange candle

*   A piece of paper 

*   Pen 

*   Lighter 

*   A bracelet or anything you want to give her/him

Hold the candle in your hand, visualize your intention and energy slowly move into it. Write your friend's full name on the piece of paper. Light the candle and say:

''Lively orange, good fortune for this person
A bright path has been made
With the eyes of intuition and spirit
Lead him/her to the right way
And he/she will deal with all obstacles with no fears
So let it be!''

Then light the candle and burn the piece of paper. Roll the bracelet on the ashes as it belongs to him/her. Place the bracelet near the candle, visualize your intention and energy are releasing by the flame and the bracelet is absorbing them. Wait for the candle to burn out. And give the bracelet to your friend.
