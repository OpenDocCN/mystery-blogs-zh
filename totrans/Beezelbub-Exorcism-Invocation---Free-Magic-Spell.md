# Beezelbub Exorcism Invocation

This spell invokes the spirit essence of Beezelbub into the Witch or practitioner to be channeled through incantation to exorcise or remove a negative energy out of a vessel, item , or space.

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Petition paper marked with a large black triangle in the center

*   A drop of Blood/Saliva of any kind; or Olive oil

*   The name Beezelbub written on black ink on a separate petition paper

*   A white or dark colored bowl (if not white preferable orange)

*   Athame or Wand

*   Holy or Sacred water (moon water, sun water, sage water, etc.)

## Casting Instructions for 'Beezelbub Exorcism Invocation'

 Place the first petition paper with the large centered black triangle in front of you in the middle of your sacred space.

Using the wand/athame call upon the spirits/energies of the cardinal directions North, West, and South (do not call upon East)

Conjure a energetic triangle around the petition paper marked with the black triangle using the wand/athame.

Place the Bowl (White, Orange, or dark colored) on top the Triangle petition.

Anoint the second petition with the name of "Beezelbub" with a drop or blood or olive oil.

If you do not have access to the first two anointing fluids; using your tongue drop saliva unto the center of "Beezelbub".

Hold the petition unto your left hand, and hold your right hand over it.

While holding visualizing a connection from yor heart chakra through your into the held petition.

Say the Verum of Beezelbub:

"I call upon though, Beezelbub, Oh Lord of Flies and rotted dust. Come forth your spirit into thy. So I may exorcise this disobedient spirit, or thine energies. I call forth Spirit of Beezelbub."

Place held petition into bowl.

Using the Athame or Wand in both hands the blade or tip pointing to the heavens. Feel or visualize the heart chakra energies through the Athame or Wand being pushed and pulled. Start to breath and hearing your breath enter a trance. Then visualize musty orange light emanating from the setup in your sacred space. Entering and forcing out the negative spirit or energies of your space, vessel, or object. Feel the vibrations of Beezelbub with in your acts and spell.

Recite mantra:

"In the name of Beezelbub, I command unclean intent out of my manifestation."

The presence of the negative energy will be removed and should be offered to Beezelbub for her/him to take away as payment.

After the spell is complete and offering given. Take your Holy or sacred water and sprinkle all over setup and chest as well as right arm and hand. Dispose of all petitions in fire. (Do not plant in gardens or graveyards therefore the connection to Beezelbub shall be opened and may or not manifest his/her desires in nature.)

If unable to burn petitions place in trash with thoughts towards the petitions of disownment.

You may clean your bowl, wand/athame in any matter you see fit.
