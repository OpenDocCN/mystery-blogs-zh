*   Full Concentration

*   A Quiet Space

Step One:

Close your eyes, and make sure there are no outside distractions.

Step Two:

Imagine four glowing orbs in your mind. One of them will be red (Fire), one of them will be blue (Water), one of them will be clear (Air) and one of them will be green (Earth).
