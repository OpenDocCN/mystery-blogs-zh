# Hex Bag of Illness: Necromancy

This spell summons the Ancestral spirits to cause spirit possession and illness to an enemy.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Hex Bag of Illness: Necromancy'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Purple Cloth

*   Common garden variety stones or pebbles

*   Nutmeg

*   Mirror

*   Ribbon or string

*   Purple Cloth

 1.  Lay flat the Purple cloth
2.  Place stones in the center of the Purple cloth.
3.  Say the hex," I chant you here and enchant whose near, with a hex of illness and fear." while sprinkling nutmeg on the stones. (With the Intent of the person in mind)
4.  Fold the Purple cloth around the stones and nutmeg.
5.  Tie the Ribbon/String around the cloth tightly, while saying, " Be bound. Oh, twine bind, and be bound."
6.  Then keep the hex bag until you are able to place or hide it in a general area your enemy visits regularly.
