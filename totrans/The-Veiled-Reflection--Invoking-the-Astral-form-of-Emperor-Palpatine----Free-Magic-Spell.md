# The Veiled Reflection: Invoking the Astral form of Emperor Palpatine. - Free Magic Spell

> 来源：[https://www.spellsofmagic.com/spells/spiritual_spells/conjuring_spells/27947/page.html#0001-01-01](https://www.spellsofmagic.com/spells/spiritual_spells/conjuring_spells/27947/page.html#0001-01-01)

 Find a secluded, dimly lit chamber devoid of external disturbances or distractions. Arrange the room in a Sith aesthetic, using dark tapestries, ancient Sith relics, and ominous artifacts.

Position the obsidian mirror at the center of the room, raised on a ceremonial pedestal, if you do not have the necessary room to do this, on a small desk or tabletop will do fine. 

Cover the mirror with the black velvet cloth, ensuring it completely shrouds the reflective surface.

Place the Sith artifacts of power around the mirror to enhance the ritual's potency.

Light the candles, (Black or Red Candles) ensuring they form a perfect circle around the mirror.

Meditate upon the dark side of the Force, drawing strength from its malevolent energy.

The Ritual:

Once the chamber is fully prepared, don the ceremonial (Sith robes; Optional) and ignite the candles, one by one.

Hold the Sith dagger aloft and speak the ancient Sith incantation, (This will require some imagination, simply make up your own invocation for invoking Palpatine). Continue invoking the power of the dark side and the presence of Emperor Palpatine.

Unveil the obsidian mirror by removing the black velvet cloth with a deliberate motion, revealing its polished surface.

Focus your intent, visualizing the image of Emperor Palpatine within the mirror, as if his spirit is contained within its depths.

Gaze deep into the mirror, allowing your mind to surrender to the darkness, opening a channel for the emperor's presence to manifest.

Empty your thoughts and open yourself to the whispers of the dark side. Let the energy flow through you, empowering your connection to the mirror.

Speak your questions, requests, or desired knowledge aloud, addressing Emperor Palpatine directly, as if he were standing before you.

Maintain your focus and let the dark side guide your actions. The mirror may shimmer, distort, or emit ominous energies, indicating the Emperor's response.

Receive the wisdom or message from Emperor Palpatine, perceiving his answers through your senses, intuition, or as audible whispers in your mind. *Keep and journal or pen and paper around you to jot down any gnosis you receive during ritual. 

Once you have communed with Emperor Palpatine and obtained the knowledge sought, express gratitude and offer a tribute of dark side energy, such as a blood or a Sith artifact.

Most importantly keep in mind that persistence, repetition, and patience are key to any magickal working, if at first you do not succeed, keep trying each night, if you feel that you are overwhelmed and that you need a break take one! But if you keep going, I know you will get results. 

May the Force serve you well! 

*Side note, if you are in a home where you are not alone and do not want others to know of your dark ritual and you prefer silence, simply get yourself a cast iron cauldron or some form of fire safe dish and write your questions to the emperor and burn them in the cauldron or dish. This will keep your ritual silent and away from prying ears.
