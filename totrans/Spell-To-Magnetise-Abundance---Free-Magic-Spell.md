# Spell To Magnetise Abundance

To bring abundance and prosperity into your life

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Spell To Magnetise Abundance'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   1 white candle and a suitable holder

*   1 green pen

*   1 lodestone (a type of magnet, available from New Age and Occult stores)

*   1 white candle and a suitable holder

*   1 green pen

*   1 lodestone (a type of magnet, available from New Age and Occult stores)

It is better to do this spell in a waxing moon phase Sit before your altar. Make sure you are comfortable.
Hold the unlit candle for a while, concentrating on the gift of abundance.
Take the green pen and write 'abundance' on one palm and 'prosperity' on the other. Don't worry about any money troubles you may have - keep your thoughts positive.
Place the lodestone before the candle and light the wick.
Postition your hands on either side of the candle, with your palms open and upwards. Close your eyes and visualise unlimited abundance flowing into your hands. Repeat the following charm, continuing for as long as you are focused and comfortable:

> I magnetise prosperity and abundance.

Let the candle burn out and go about your day.
