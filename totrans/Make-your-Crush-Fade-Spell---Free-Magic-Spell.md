# Make your Crush Fade Spell

When you are in love with someone but you don't want to be, use this spell.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Make your Crush Fade Spell'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Image of the person you have a crush on

*   Fire

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Image of the person you have a crush on

*   Fire

 Light a fire in a place where it won't burn anything down. Take the image of the person you have a crush on and carefully put it in the fire. Chant:

"I have loved you for way too long.

When you're around, my heart beats like a song.

I wish that I never did love you.

As I throw your picture into fire as red as a blood moon."

Nothing will happen at first, but when you wake up in the morning, you will find that you don't love the person anymore.
