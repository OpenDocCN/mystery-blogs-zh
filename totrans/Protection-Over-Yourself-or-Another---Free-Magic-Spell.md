# Protection Over Yourself or Another

  Rated 5/5 Stars

This is a protection charm for yourself or another of choice. It is meant to be worn on person or in a bag, purse etc.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Protection Over Yourself or Another'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   1 or more pieces of paper

*   1 pen

*   1 candle of your choice

*   1 lighter or matches

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   1 or more pieces of paper

*   1 pen

*   1 candle of your choice

*   1 lighter or matches

 Get the piece of paper and write the following in order,

"Protect me
From harm
From evil/good
From those who
I wish not to see"

Then fold it into an envolope by foldind the bottom of the sheet just over three quarters of the way or until you can fold the top piece over as a closer. Now then draw a pentagram in the middle of the outside (under the fold) and swipe it threw the flame of the candle making sure it dosn't catch alight

Chant what is on the sheet of paper 3x. Now fold the paper again into an envolope this time write your name (or the persons you are making it for) under the fold and do the same with the flame and the chant and then fold it one last time into an envolope draw another pentagram and say 3x: "protect me"

Then either wear this on you until it is not needed or put it into a pocket, bag, purse, wallet etc.
