# Vampiric House Creation Spell

This is a spell for when you create a vampiric house, which is a group of psi and/or sanguine vampires that practice together/help each other practice.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Vampiric House Creation Spell'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   The people who will be in the vampiric house.

*   Candles [Optional]

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   The people who will be in the vampiric house.

*   Candles [Optional]

 First, link hands with the house members to form a circle. Have the High Priestess/Priest stand in the middle, or if there is not one just say the chant as a group.

Chant: "The house we wish to form,

             brings us together now.

             We say this chant that will conform

             with the desires of our house.

            [House name] will bind us,

            Together we'll be one.

            To help and aid each other,

            Until the dying of the sun."

[If you chose to light candles, put them out at the end of the chant.]
