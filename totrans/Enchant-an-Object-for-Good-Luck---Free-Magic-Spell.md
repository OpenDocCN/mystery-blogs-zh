# Enchant an Object for Good Luck

This spell will allow you to enchant an object/clothing (preferably a ring or necklace) to give you good luck while you are around it.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Enchant an Object for Good Luck'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Object you want to enchant

*   Large white candle

*   Red candle

*   Pink candle

*   Green candle

*   Incense and Incense Burner

*   Matches

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Object you want to enchant

*   Large white candle

*   Red candle

*   Pink candle

*   Green candle

*   Incense and Incense Burner

*   Matches

1\. Light the incense and put it in the burner (NOTE: You must use matches)
2\. Put a large white candle in front of you.
3\. Place the red, pink and green candles in a triangular shape around the white candle and light them.
4\. Hold your object and chant as many times as it takes:
''Bless this (name of object) as a token of magic,
Protecting me from spirits so tragic.
Bring good luck and fortune to me,
Bring good luck, so mote it be!''
5\. Wear or hold your object and gather the energy to nake a chi ball, but you do not actually need to make one.
6\. Say, ''Good Luck'' and imagine your Aura going into the object.
7\. Continue to hold/wear your object for a few minutes, and then blow out the candles.
8\. Put the incense to the side and let it burn out.

If you wish, you can put the incense near a peice of Amythest.
