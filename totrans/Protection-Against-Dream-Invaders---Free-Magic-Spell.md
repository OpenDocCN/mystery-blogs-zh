# Protection Against Dream Invaders

  Rated 5/5 Stars

There are some people who can invade your dreams, who can travel astrally to your home, or even drain your energy while you sleep.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Protection Against Dream Invaders'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Fresh Apple Seeds

*   White Cloth Pouch

*   Safety Pin

*   Your Pillow

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Fresh Apple Seeds

*   White Cloth Pouch

*   Safety Pin

*   Your Pillow

 Wrap three fresh apple seeds in a white cloth pouch. Tie the pouch closed. Don't lose the
seeds. Safety pin the pouch to the pillow you sleep on. Sleep with the pouch attached to your pillow for seven nights. At the end of the seven nights, remove the three seeds from the pouch and toss them out your front door. On the off chance that a spirit is leaving it's body and traveling to your home, this will make it impossible for it to enter the house.
