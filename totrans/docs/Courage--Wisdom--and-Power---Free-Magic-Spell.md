# Courage, Wisdom, and Power

Gain courage, wisdom and power.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Courage, Wisdom, and Power'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   1 Red candle

*   1 Purple candle

*   1 White candle

*   1 Silk rope (any length or thickness)

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   1 Red candle

*   1 Purple candle

*   1 White candle

*   1 Silk rope (any length or thickness)

 Cast your circle. Light the candles and lay them on the rope on your alter. Chant as you tie your knots in the rope.

"With this rope I bind thine power,
To be mine for a second or hour.
To make me strong when I am weak.
To give me wisdom that I seek.
To give me courage as not to flee.
Thine will be done, so mote it be."

Keep the rope with or near you to recieve the power and courage you requested.
