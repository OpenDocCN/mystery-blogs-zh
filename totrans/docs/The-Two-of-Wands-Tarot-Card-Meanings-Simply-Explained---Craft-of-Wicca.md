## The Two of Wands tarot card description

The Two of Wands tarot card typically features an image of a man holding a globe in one hand and a staff or wand in the other.

 He is standing on a balcony or terrace, often with a city or landscape in the background. The globe represents the world and the man’s ability to control it, while the staff or wand symbolizes his power and authority.

 The image suggests that the man is in a position of leadership and has the ability to make important decisions that will affect his future.

 Some versions of the card also have a small ship in the distance, representing an upcoming journey or a new opportunity. In some decks, the card depicts a figure holding a wand or a staff, looking into the distance with a sense of determination and focus as if planning and visualizing a future opportunity or undertaking.

### Astrology of the Two of Wands card

In tarot, each card is associated with a specific astrological sign or planet. The Two of Wands is often associated with the astrological sign of Aries.

 Aries is a fire sign, known for its energy, assertiveness, and leadership qualities. The Two of Wands represents someone who is taking the initiative, making plans, and taking control of their future. They have the confidence and determination to turn their vision into a reality.

 This card can also be associated with the planet Mars, which is also associated with Aries and is known for its association with action, courage, and the desire to win.

In summary, the Two of Wands represents the start of a new venture, the need for leadership, and the ability to plan ahead with confidence and determination. This card often signifies a time of expansion, new opportunities, and the desire to take action toward achieving one’s goals.

## Upright Two of Wands tarot card meaning

What is the meaning of the upright Two of Wands card?

 **The upright Two of Wands tarot card represents an individual who is at a crossroads, making important decisions and planning for the future. It represents ambition, confidence, and control, indicating that the individual is moving forward in the right direction and embarking on new opportunities and adventures.**

The traditional interpretation of the upright Two of Wands tarot card is that the individual is at a crossroads, considering their options and making important decisions about their future. They are in a position of power and have the ability to make things happen.

The card suggests that the individual is ambitious and has a clear vision for their future. They are confident in their ability to succeed and are willing to take risks to achieve their goals.

 The Two of Wands can also indicate that the individual is ready to embark on a new journey or adventure, whether it be in their personal or professional life.

 The card also represents that individual is likely to be in control of their situation and have the ability to make choices and plan for the future. The Two of Wands is a very positive card, indicating that the individual is making progress and moving forward in the right direction.

### Example upright Two of Wands reading

A real-world example of tarot reading for the upright Two of Wands could be for an individual who is considering starting their own business.

 The Two of Wands suggests that this individual has a clear vision for their future and the ambition and determination to make it happen. They are in a position of power and control and are confident in their ability to succeed.

The card advises these individuals to consider their options carefully and to take action toward achieving their goals. It also indicates that this new venture will likely bring new opportunities and a sense of adventure.

 Another example could be an individual who is considering moving to a new city or country.

 The Two of Wands suggests that this individual is at a crossroads, considering their options and making important decisions about their future. The card indicates that the individual is ambitious, has a clear vision for their future, and is confident in their ability to make the move happen.

The Two of Wands advises this individual to take action and to be ready for new opportunities and adventures that will come with the move.

## Reversed Two of Wands card tarot meaning

What is the meaning of the reversed Two of Wands tarot card?

 **The reversed Two of Wands tarot card represents an individual who may be struggling with decision-making and moving forward. They feel uncertain and lack control, experiencing delays or obstacles in their plans and lacking direction. They are too passive, indecisive, or procrastinate on important decisions.**

The traditional interpretation of the reversed Two of Wands tarot card is that the individual may be struggling with making decisions and moving forward.

 They may be feeling uncertain or hesitant about their plans and future. They may also be experiencing a lack of control or power in their current situation. The card can indicate that the individual is not ready to take action or is unable to see the bigger picture.

 They may be feeling stagnant and unproductive or lacking direction.

On a more negative note, the reversed Two of Wands can suggest that the individual is being too passive, indecisive, or procrastinating on important decisions, and not taking responsibility for their own future.

 This card can also suggest that the individual may be facing obstacles or delays in their plans and they need to re-evaluate and make adjustments to their strategies.

 ### Example reversed Two of Wands reading

A real-world example of a tarot reading for the reversed Two of Wands tarot card could be for an individual who is struggling with making a decision about whether to commit to their current relationship.

The reversed Two of Wands suggests that this individual may be feeling uncertain or hesitant about their future with their partner. They may be experiencing a lack of control or power in their relationship.

The card advises this individual to take a step back and evaluate the situation carefully. They may need to communicate with their partner and work through any issues that are causing uncertainty.

 It’s important for these individuals to be honest with themselves and their partners about their feelings, and take responsibility for their own happiness.

 Another example could be an individual who is experiencing delays or obstacles in their plans to move in together or get engaged.

The reversed Two of Wands suggests that this individual may be feeling stagnant and unproductive in their relationship. They may be facing financial or family issues that are causing delays or obstacles.

 The card advises this individual to re-evaluate and make adjustments to their strategies. They may need to come up with a plan to overcome these obstacles and work together with their partner to make the plans happen.
