## The Six of Wands tarot card description

The Six of Wands tarot card is a triumph card that symbolizes victory, accomplishment, and success.

Typically, the image on the card depicts a central figure, often a horseman or knight, holding a staff or wand with six others arranged behind him.

 The horseman is often depicted with a look of pride or confidence, while the people behind him hold their own wands, symbolizing their support. The horseman’s laurel wreath or crown is a symbol of achievement and recognition.

In some interpretations, the six wands symbolize the six directions or the triumph of the central figure over his opponents in different directions. A city or castle may also appear in the background to suggest stability and security after a victory.

 Overall, the imagery on the Six of Wands tarot card is meant to evoke a sense of pride, accomplishment, and recognition of one’s efforts by others.

### Astrology of the Six of Wands card

In tarot, each card can have multiple astrological associations, but typically the Six of Wands is associated with the astrological sign of Leo.

 Leo is a fire sign known for its courage, confidence, and generosity.

These qualities align with the themes of triumph and recognition depicted on the Six of Wands card. Leo’s natural rulership over the 5th house of creativity, self-expression, and children also ties in with the idea of success and achievement represented by the card.

 It is important to note that tarot and astrology are separate systems of divination, and the astrological associations with tarot cards can vary based on the practitioner or deck being used.

 ## Upright Six of Wands tarot card meaning

What is the meaning of the upright Six of Wands card?

**The upright Six of Wands in an upright position signifies triumph and success, predicting recognition, validation, and a positive change in the person’s circumstances. It suggests the person is confident, proud, and leading the way with support from others.**

 The Six of Wands in an upright position symbolizes triumph, victory, and success. It suggests that the person receiving the reading is likely to experience recognition and validation for their efforts, and they may feel proud and confident as a result.

 This card indicates that the person is likely to experience a positive change in their circumstances, whether it be in their personal or professional life. This change could come in the form of a promotion, an award, or even public recognition.

The Six of Wands suggests that the person is leading the way and others are following and supporting them. They may have a strong sense of self-assurance and believe in their ability to succeed. This card can also indicate a time of celebration and recognition for their hard work.

 Overall, the upright Six of Wands is a positive and encouraging card that predicts success, recognition, and a sense of pride and accomplishment for the person receiving the reading.

 ### Example upright Six of Wands reading

Peter wants to start running for exercise and he draws the upright Six of Wands card during a reading. What does this mean?

The upright Six of Wands card in this scenario suggests that Peter’s decision to start running for exercise will likely lead to a positive change and personal triumph.

 The card signifies that Peter may experience a sense of pride and confidence in his ability to succeed, and he will likely be met with support and recognition for his efforts. This bodes well for Peter’s running goals and overall health and wellness.

 ## Reversed Six of Wands card tarot meaning

What is the meaning of the reversed Six of Wands tarot card?

**The reversed Six of Wands suggests a lack of confidence and support, indicating obstacles, setbacks, and criticism in the person’s path to success. It can also indicate feelings of inadequacy, self-doubt, and opposition, but encourages the person to find the strength to overcome these challenges and achieve their goals.**

 The Six of Wands in a reversed position can indicate a lack of confidence or a feeling of being unsupported. It suggests that the person may be experiencing obstacles or setbacks in their path to success and recognition.

 They may feel like their efforts are not being recognized or validated, leading to a sense of disappointment and frustration.

The reversed Six of Wands can also indicate a lack of leadership or feeling overwhelmed by the competition. The person may feel like they are falling behind or losing out to others, leading to feelings of inadequacy and self-doubt.

 This card can also suggest that the person may be facing criticism or opposition, either from others or themselves. They may feel like they are not meeting their own expectations or that they are being held back in some way.

 Overall, the reversed Six of Wands can indicate a period of challenge and difficulty, but it also encourages the person to find the strength and confidence within themselves to overcome these obstacles and achieve their goals.

### Example reversed Six of Wands reading

Christina asks the tarot about continuing with her failed acting career. She draws the reversed Six of Wands. What does this mean?

 The reversed Six of Wands in this scenario suggests that Christina may be facing challenges and obstacles in her acting career. She may be feeling unconfident and unsupported, and her efforts may not be recognized or validated. This can lead to disappointment and frustration.

 The reversed Six of Wands can also indicate that Christina is feeling overwhelmed by the competition and that she may be facing opposition or criticism, either from others or herself. She may feel like she is not meeting her own expectations or that she is being held back in some way.

Overall, the reversed Six of Wands suggests that Christina’s acting career may not be going as well as she would like, but it also encourages her to find the strength and confidence within herself to overcome these obstacles and achieve her goals.

 ## Meanings of the Six of Wands in tarot positions

Depending on the position of the Six of Wands card, you will need to interpret it differently.

 ### Love Six of Wands tarot meaning

In a love context, the Six of Wands can indicate a time of victory and triumph in the person’s romantic life. They may experience recognition and validation for their efforts in their relationship, and they may feel proud and confident as a result.

This card suggests that the person is likely to experience a positive change in their love life, such as an engagement, a wedding, or even a public recognition of their love and commitment.

 The Six of Wands can indicate that the person is leading the way in the relationship and their partner is supportive and following.

 Overall, the Six of Wands in a love context is a positive and encouraging card that predicts success, recognition, and a sense of pride and accomplishment in the person’s romantic life.

### Career Six of Wands tarot meaning

In a career context, the Six of Wands can indicate a time of triumph and success. The person may experience recognition and validation for their efforts at work, and they may feel proud and confident as a result.

 This card suggests that the person is likely to experience a positive change in their career, such as a promotion, a successful project, or even a public recognition of their achievements.

 The Six of Wands can indicate that the person is leading the way in their field and their colleagues are supportive and following.

Overall, the Six of Wands in a career context is a positive and encouraging card that predicts success, recognition, and a sense of pride and accomplishment in the person’s professional life.

 ### Finances Six of Wands tarot meaning

In a financial context, the Six of Wands can indicate a time of financial triumph and success. The person may experience recognition and validation for their efforts in managing their finances, and they may feel proud and confident as a result.

 This card suggests that the person is likely to experience a positive change in their financial situation, such as an increase in income, a successful investment, or even a public recognition of their financial skills.

The Six of Wands can indicate that the person is leading the way in their financial management and their financial situation is supportive and secure.

 Overall, the Six of Wands in a financial context is a positive and encouraging card that predicts financial success, recognition, and a sense of pride and accomplishment in the person’s financial life.
