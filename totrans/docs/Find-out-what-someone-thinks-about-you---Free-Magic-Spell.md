# Find out what someone thinks about you

Learn what someone things about you simply in a dream.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Find out what someone thinks about you'

Place under pillow, right before bed, Chant: “With dreamers eyes I seek to see what this person thinks of me. Be it good or be it ill, reveal to me as is my will.” Then close your eyes and imagine far across a room filled with people gossiping about you, and the person.
