# Protection/Luck Necklace Enchantment

For protection and luck!

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Protection/Luck Necklace Enchantment'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Basil

*   Rosemary

*   Chamomile

*   Peppermint

*   Spring water

*   Small spray bottle or Jar

*   Necklace with pendant

*   1 small green candle (for luck)

*   1 small blue candle (for protection)

(Before anything else leave the herbs in a bottle of spring water over night to be blessed by the moon)

Sit in a dark room and light the green and blue candle in front of you
Take the necklace in hand and chant the following three times. ''Bless this necklace, charm it well. Give the wearer good luck, make it swell. Bless all around them and bless the wearer, may protection spread and rid the terror. In the name of the divine goddess, As I will it, so mote it be.''
Then spray/soak the pendant in your herb water concoction and snuff out the candles. You're all set. Blessed be!
