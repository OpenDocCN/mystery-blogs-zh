*   Water

 This enchantment will allow your amulets or charms to function or have power drawn from a longer distance, One room no matter the size.

1) Place charm or amulet in the center of the cloth.
2) Sprinkle around one teaspoon of salt on the Amulet or Charm
3) Shower the amulet or charm in pepper, no specific amount is required, but still use it.
4) Sprinkle the about one tablespoon of ground white pepper on the charm or amulet.
5) Fold up the cloth with materials in it.
6) Hold it under running water for 30 seconds.
7) Durring this, chant;

Oh charm, sweet charm, covered in pepper and salt. May the power I draw from you not come to a halt. For as long as a door shall not separate our path in time, your power I draw may be still mine . Your functions shall still work like most eyes can see, please give me some power, so mote it be.
