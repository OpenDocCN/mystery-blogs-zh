# The Everlasting Immortality Spell No.1

  Rated 5/5 Stars

This is based on the pentacle and the gods of immortality to call upon them to confer immorality. This uses the necklace for the spell.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'The Everlasting Immortality Spell No.1'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   5 Candles

*   Necklace (Preferably an egyptian style necklace but any fancy necklace can do)

*   Altar

*   Paper

*   Blessed Pen and Ink

*   Incense

*   Voice

*   Full Moon

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   5 Candles

*   Necklace (Preferably an egyptian style necklace but any fancy necklace can do)

*   Altar

*   Paper

*   Blessed Pen and Ink

*   Incense

*   Voice

*   Full Moon

Set the candles around the altar, White at the top and the elemental colors in their respective places.

Light the incense and open the circle. Call upon the God/Goddess of immortality.

Light the candles and place the necklace on the altar.

Write down the physical age you want to stay at with the pen and ink.

Then say

God/Goddess of Immortality, grant me the wish I wish for truly, then make me stay at my age of youth.

Pass the paper around the flames of each candle (don't burn it!) and say

God/Goddess of Immortality, make this spell come true, to really bless my dream be new.

Dispose of the paper by burning in the flames and say ''So mote it be''.

Leave the necklace in the moon for the cycle until new moon.

Then wear it.
