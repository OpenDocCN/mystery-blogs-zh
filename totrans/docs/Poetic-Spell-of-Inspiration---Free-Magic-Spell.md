# Poetic Spell of Inspiration

  Rated 5/5 Stars

For poets to give themselves a muse.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Poetic Spell of Inspiration'

**You will need the following items for this spell:****You will need the following items for this spell:**Perform on a Wednesday when the moon is waxing. Best if done when the moon is in Gemini, but it's not required.

Light a purple candle. You can also burn some jasmine or rose incense if you wish.

Say this incantation:

> Oh Goddess Brigit,
> bright lady of the flame,
> blessed mother of the poets.
> Lend me such insight, wisdom, and divine inspiration
> that the words may flow to me
> and that I may may craft them beautifully
> so that they spin a tapestry of magic
> In the hearts of all who hear them.
> So mote it be!

Leave the candle to burn itself out. when it does, put it wherever you keep rituals remnants and give thanks to the Goddess for her gift. From - Phantom120
