# See if He/She Loves You or Not

This is a spell to find out if your lover loves you or not.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'See if He/She Loves You or Not'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Piece of paper(as big as a notebook).

*   Pink crayon

*   Silver/Golden sparkles

*   Pink sparkles(mostly needed)

*   Glue

*   Bigger plate

*   Sugar

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Piece of paper(as big as a notebook).

*   Pink crayon

*   Silver/Golden sparkles

*   Pink sparkles(mostly needed)

*   Glue

*   Bigger plate

*   Sugar

 All you have to do with the piece of paper is to cut it in shapes of hearts as big as your hand. Make 2 hearts. Then you need to colour them in pink. Now put glue from the sides and put on pink sparkles. In the midd of the hearts write your and your beloved's names(one name on each heart). After you do this you put them in water. Make sure they are whole in the water. Then add the sugar in the water. Next morning check the hearts. If your heart is more damaged, you do not love him enough. If his/her heart is more damaged that means he/she does not love you much. You make this at night. Next morning first thing you do is to get the hearts out of the water.
