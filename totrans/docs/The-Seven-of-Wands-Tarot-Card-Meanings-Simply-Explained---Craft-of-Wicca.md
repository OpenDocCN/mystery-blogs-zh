## The Seven of Wands tarot card description

The Seven of Wands tarot card often depicts a person defending themselves against several attackers.

 The person is typically shown holding a wand, ready to fight off their opponents.

 The attackers are often depicted as men armed with clubs or wands, attacking from all sides. In the background, there may be a battlefield or a wall, symbolizing the challenges and obstacles that the person must overcome.

 The overall imagery conveys a message of determination, bravery, and the courage to defend oneself against multiple challenges and adversaries.

### Astrology of the Seven of Wands card

In tarot, the Seven of Wands is often associated with the astrological sign of Mars in Leo.

 Mars represents energy, drive, and determination, while Leo represents courage and confidence. The combination of these two energies symbolizes the determination and bravery to stand up for oneself and defend one’s position, even in the face of opposition.

 This card can indicate a need to be assertive, to stand up for oneself, and to face challenges and competition with courage and resilience.

## Upright Seven of Wands tarot card meaning

What is the meaning of the upright Seven of Wands card?

**The upright Seven of Wands signifies a time of conflict and adversity, representing a need to be assertive and defend one’s beliefs and position. This card calls for courage and determination, encouraging the individual to face challenges head-on and prove their resilience in overcoming obstacles.**

 The Seven of Wands in the upright position symbolizes a moment of tumultuous strife and adversity.

It suggests that one is up against a barrage of opposition and must be ready to fortify their stance and defend their beliefs with unwavering determination.

This card demands a bold and assertive demeanor, encouraging one to assert their own voice and not back down from any challenges that come their way. This is a call to action, to take the reins of power and assert oneself in the face of opposition, facing every challenge head-on with courage and tenacity.

 The Seven of Wands represents a time to channel inner resilience and strength, rise above the fray, and prove that one is capable of overcoming even the most formidable obstacles.

 ### Example upright Seven of Wands reading

Tamara asks the tarot about her health and draws the upright Seven of Wands. What does this mean?

The upright Seven of Wands in tarot can suggest a time of challenges and obstacles, even in regard to one’s health.

 It may indicate a need to be assertive and proactive in taking care of oneself, and to stand up for one’s own health and well-being in the face of any opposition or obstacles. This card may also suggest that one must be prepared to face multiple health challenges, but with determination and resilience.

Overall, the Seven of Wands in this context can represent the courage to face any health challenges head-on and to overcome them with determination and a strong will.

 ## Reversed Seven of Wands card tarot meaning

What is the meaning of the reversed Seven of Wands tarot card?

 **The reversed Seven of Wands suggests a lack of confidence and a tendency to avoid conflict, indicating a need to regain assertiveness and stand up for oneself in the face of challenges. This card warns against becoming passive and encourages the individual to rediscover inner strength and resilience to overcome obstacles and pursue their goals.**

The Seven of Wands reversed speaks to a wavering spirit and a tendency to shy away from conflict.

It suggests a lack of resilience and an inability to stand tall in the face of opposition, leaving one feeling crushed and defeated by the weight of life’s challenges.

 This card warns against becoming passive, ceding control to others, and surrendering one’s own power to outside forces. Instead, it calls for the rekindling of courage and determination, urging one to reclaim their voice and take back control of their own fate.

The Seven of Wands reversed speaks to a moment of introspection, to dig deep and rediscover the inner strength required to overcome any obstacle and rise above adversity.

 ### Example reversed Seven of Wands reading

Terry wants to take time off to travel the world. He asks the tarot about this and draws the reversed Seven of Wands. What does this mean?

 The reversed Seven of Wands in tarot can indicate a lack of confidence or a tendency to shy away from pursuing one’s goals, even in regard to travel.

 This card may suggest that Terry is feeling overwhelmed by obstacles and opposition, or that he is allowing others to dictate his actions, leading to a sense of being stuck.

The reversed Seven of Wands can also suggest that Terry is avoiding confrontation, and not taking the necessary steps to make his travel dreams a reality.

 This card may indicate a need for Terry to regain his confidence, stand up for himself, and to face any challenges head-on in order to pursue his travel goals.

The Seven of Wands reversed can represent a time for Terry to rediscover his inner strength and resilience, and to take action to make his travel dreams a reality.

## Meanings of the Seven of Wands in tarot positions

Depending on the position of the Seven of Wands card, you will need to interpret it differently.

 ### Love Seven of Wands tarot meaning

In a love context, the Seven of Wands in tarot can suggest a time of challenges and competition. This card may indicate that the individual is feeling challenged in their relationship and is facing opposition from others who may be vying for their affection.

The Seven of Wands can suggest that the individual must be assertive and stand up for their relationship, fight for their love, and defend their commitment to their partner.

This card may also indicate a need to be proactive in building and strengthening the relationship, overcoming any obstacles, and proving their resilience and determination in love.

 The Seven of Wands in love can represent a time to face challenges with courage and determination and to show that the bond between partners is unbreakable.

### Career Seven of Wands tarot meaning

In a career context, the Seven of Wands in tarot can suggest a time of challenges and competition.

 This card may indicate that the individual is facing opposition in their work, and is feeling the pressure to defend their position and prove their worth.

 The Seven of Wands can suggest that the individual must be assertive and stand up for themselves, to fight for their success and defend their achievements.

This card may also indicate a need to be proactive in building and strengthening their professional reputation, to overcome any obstacles and to prove their resilience and determination in their career.

The Seven of Wands in a career context can represent a time to face challenges with courage and determination, and to show that the individual is capable of success and achievement in their field.

 ### Finances Seven of Wands tarot meaning

In a financial context, the Seven of Wands in tarot can suggest a time of challenges and competition. This card may indicate that the individual is facing obstacles in their finances and is feeling pressure to defend their financial stability.

 The Seven of Wands can suggest that the individual must be assertive and take control of their finances, to fight for their financial security and defend their resources.

This card may also indicate a need to be proactive in building and strengthening their financial foundation, to overcome any obstacles and to prove their resilience and determination in their finances.

 The Seven of Wands in a financial context can represent a time to face challenges with courage and determination, and to show that the individual is capable of managing their finances and securing their financial future.

## Advanced modern tarot interpretations of the Seven of Wands card

I always like to examine other, modern decks to see what can be read into their symbolism.

The reason for this is that every person will have different decks, so it’s good to learn how to go with your gut on what you see. This is the best way to have a good interpretation of the moment.

 Too many people stick to a learned reading instead of going with the flow. Remember, each reading, deck, person, position, and question is different. Reading the cards is an art, not a science.

Here is a new interpretation of the Seven of Wands tarot card:

 The Seven of Wands indicates that the individual is facing obstacles and opposition in their technological ventures, and is feeling the pressure to defend their position and prove their worth.

 The Seven of Wands can suggest that the individual must be assertive, innovative, and quick on their feet, to stay ahead of the curve and defend their technological advancements.

This card may also indicate a need to be proactive in adapting to new technology, to overcome any obstacles and to prove their resilience and determination in this ever-evolving field.

 The Seven of Wands in a technological context can represent a time to face challenges with creativity, agility, and determination, and to show that the individual is a leader in the technological arena.
