# Wish Someone a Safe Travel

If someone you like is going on a trip this is a great spell to give them safety during there traveling.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Wish Someone a Safe Travel'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   White candles anointed with sandalwood oil

*   1 purple candle anointed with sandalwood oil

*   Photo or personal articles of the person

*   ''Personality'' candle

*   Sandalwood incense
