# Spell to Bring Forgiveness

  Rated 5/5 Stars

This spell will help heal the hurts of unforgivenss and help to bring about healing.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Spell to Bring Forgiveness'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Bunch Parsley

*   Bowl of Water

*   White Ribbon

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Bunch Parsley

*   Bowl of Water

*   White Ribbon

 Tie ribbon round parsley bunch. Dip parsley in water and then sprinkle the water over yourself using the bunch of parsley. While you do this chant:

> **"I wash away all hurt and pain
> I wash away deep wounds like rain
> All cross words and guilt depart
> Let deep forgiveness heal my heart
> Hang the parsley over your bed while you sleep. In the morning bury it along with all your pain."**
