# Reverse Negativity or Hexes

  Rated 5/5 Stars

This spell is designed to help reduce negativity or hexes that have been worked upon you.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Reverse Negativity or Hexes'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Purple candle

*   Rosemary oil

*   White paper

*   Blank ink

*   Bowl

*   Ashtray

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Purple candle

*   Rosemary oil

*   White paper

*   Blank ink

*   Bowl

*   Ashtray

 Try this spell to reverse any negativity or hexes being sent your way. Visualize all blocks in your path being removed. Anoint a purple candle with the rosemary oil. Then, on a piece of white paper write the following in black ink: "All blocks are now removed".

Fold the paper three times. Light the candle and burn the paper in a bowl, ashtray, or any fireproof dish. Invoke the power of fire and its elemental spirits by repeating three times: "Fire drakes and salamanders, aid me in my quest. Protect me from all evil forms. Turn back the negativity being sent". After the third repetition, say "so mote it be".
