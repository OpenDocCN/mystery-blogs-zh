 ## The Gods and Goddesses of Norse Wicca

The pantheon of Norse Wicca includes a wide range of deities, each with its own unique characteristics and attributes. The most prominent of these gods and goddesses include Odin, Thor, Freyja, and Freyr.

 Odin, also known as the Allfather, is the ruler of the gods and the god of war, wisdom, and magic. He is often depicted as a wise and powerful ruler, who is able to see into the future and who is able to shape the course of events through his wisdom and cunning.

Thor is the god of thunder and is often depicted as a powerful and fierce warrior, who wields a mighty hammer called Mjolnir. He is the protector of the gods and is often invoked for protection and strength in battle.

 Freyja is the goddess of love, fertility, and war. She is often depicted as a beautiful and powerful goddess, who is able to grant prosperity and abundance to those who worship her.

 Freyr is the god of fertility and is often depicted as a powerful and fertile god, who is able to grant wealth and prosperity to those who worship him.
