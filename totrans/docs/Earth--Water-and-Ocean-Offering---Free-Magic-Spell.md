# Earth, Water and Ocean Offering

If you have a core element and it is either water or earth, use this to give back to the gods and goddesses of your element. It will make them more willing when you are casting spells

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Earth, Water and Ocean Offering'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Water

*   Salt (to strengthen the spell and to call to the water)

*   5 different signs of nature (can be any kind of plant but preferably leafs and flowers. For the flower color symbolization see the instructions.

*   Some sort of bowl (if you have a cauldron that works best)

*   Something to stir with

Part of it to call to the sea gods and godesses and part to strengthen the spell. After that individualy put in each symbol of nature and continuously stir the mixture till all of them are in. Then recite this spell.

''Gods and goddesses of Earth, Water and sea, come to me and except this offering. Please hear my plea and protect me. Make my magic stronger so mote it be.''
