# Shikigami: Centipede Spirit

A spell used to conjure a shikigami in the form of a Centipede for it to gain the creatures aspects. This spell uses the centipede spirit to remove a lover from a target in order to improve personal social matters in your life.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Shikigami: Centipede Spirit'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   White Petition paper

*   Paprika

*   Cauldron

*   Black sharpie

*   Olive oil

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   White Petition paper

*   Paprika

*   Cauldron

*   Black sharpie

*   Olive oil

 1.  Write the names of the couple on the petition paper, the X out the partner you want to remove.
2.  Place Paprika into cauldron enough to cover the bottom.
3.  Anoint the petition with Olive oil in the center; then use your hands to rub in the intent of the olive oil into the petition.
4.  Roll petition around in paprika from the cauldron.
5.  Fold petition twice towards you turning it clockwise each time you fold.
6.  Place into cauldron and say te following words and names in the form of a incantation.

Incantation:

*   "Oh, Centipede spirit, oh great insect, To remove with, to remove this, that whom lover shall not be with, (full name of the partner you want to get rid of)."

Place cauldron on altar until spell manifests for you.
