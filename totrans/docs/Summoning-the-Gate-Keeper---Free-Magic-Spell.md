# Summoning the Gate Keeper

  Rated 3/5 Stars

This ritual binds you with Satan forever.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Summoning the Gate Keeper'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   2 blue candles

*   2 black candles

*   1 red candle

*   Fennel

*   Pine

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   2 blue candles

*   2 black candles

*   1 red candle

*   Fennel

*   Pine

 On the night of the new moon go to a woodland and cast a circle and build a altar of stone or wood. Let it set for 1 moon phase. Return to the circle and draw a pentacle in the circle and place the candles as follow from the point facing down: place the red candle, place the blue candles on the middle points, and the black candles on the upper points. Place the pine and fennel on the altar and burn them take in some of the incense. Say:

"I call to the keeper,
He who guards lost souls
Hear me now
Show me the gates
Let me enter
And take my place
In Satan's army"
