# Mirror Box Spell (Return to sender)

This spell is to send back all negative energy sent your way

*   - A small or medium sized box or Tupperware

*   - A small paper

*   - A black pen

*   - 4 pieces of a mirror (size does not matter as long as they fit inside the box

*   - tape of glue
