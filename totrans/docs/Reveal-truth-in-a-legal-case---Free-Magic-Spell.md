# Reveal truth in a legal case

Ensure the truth comes out in a legal case; specifically designed for defendants who are being wrongfully tried/targeted. Be careful, NOT for use to swing a case in your favor where you are trying to manipulate the case and are actually in the wrong.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Reveal truth in a legal case'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Dragon's blood incense

*   Black candle

*   Yellow candle

*   Plaintiff's name written on piece of paper

*   Consecrated water (moon or storm water is good)

*   Black salt

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Dragon's blood incense

*   Black candle

*   Yellow candle

*   Plaintiff's name written on piece of paper

*   Consecrated water (moon or storm water is good)

*   Black salt

 1\. Cast a circle if this is part of your practice

2\. Light the dragon's blood incense and meditate on the facts of the case that need to be made clear

3\. Write the opposing party's name on a small piece of paper, place in front of the black candle

4\. Light the black candle and say "In the truth is power, in the truth is strength"

5\. Light the yellow candle on the black candle and say, "I'm protected by the truth, the facts are on my side."

6\. Pass the paper through the incense smoke and say "in the name of air, your lies have no power."

7\. Light the paper with the yellow candle and let it burn in your cauldron or a cast iron skillet (for safety).  Say "in the name of fire, your lies have no power."  Let the paper burn away

8\. Pour the storm water on the ashes and state "in the name of water, your lies have no power."

9\. Sprinkle the black salt on the ashes and say "in the name of earth, your lies have no power."

10\. Finish by saying "in the name of the elements, the truth will prevail.  I stand protected in the light with the universe on my side.  So mote it be."

11\. Open the circle if this is in your practice.  Wipe out the remains of from the cast iron skillet and dispose of them.  Keep the yellow candle on your altar, if you have to go to court and you have used a small bit of candle you can take it with you to court as well.

Good luck!
