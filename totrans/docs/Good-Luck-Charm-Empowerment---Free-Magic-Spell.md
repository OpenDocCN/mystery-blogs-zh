# Good Luck Charm Empowerment

Need some extra good luck or something that means something to you help you in your magic by giving you belief, energy and strength? Well look no more.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Good Luck Charm Empowerment'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Paper

*   Any Leaf

*   Bowl or Cup of Water

*   Candle

*   The item you want to empower

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Paper

*   Any Leaf

*   Bowl or Cup of Water

*   Candle

*   The item you want to empower

 Sit crossed legged on the floor or a flat, strong surface. Place the paper in front of you. Draw a symbol in the middle (any that has some meaning like a Zodiac symbol ect.) Then place the charm in the middle of the symbol. Cup you hands over the charm + symbol. Close your eyes.

Chant:
"Put luck upon thee,
create my power unto thee,
make it so energy is made to assist me in thee."
5x
If you believe then it should be empowered and lucky.

Then tear the leaf into the water.

Cup your hands over the water and say
"Water Empower thee..."
10x
Then drop the charm in the water and stirr.

Then say:
"Water,wind,earth and fire,
bring me what I so desire,
make this charm as lucky as can be,
so it can help thee."
once.

Then take the charm out of the water. It should now be lucky.
