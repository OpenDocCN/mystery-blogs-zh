# Contacting a Friend Spell

This spell should be used to cause someone with whom you have lost contact to contact you.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Contacting a Friend Spell'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   White candle anointed w/ sandalwood oil

*   Sandalwood incense

*   Photo of the person

*   Small glass of water

*   Salt

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   White candle anointed w/ sandalwood oil

*   Sandalwood incense

*   Photo of the person

*   Small glass of water

*   Salt

Light the candle and the incense. Place the picture of the person on your altar. Put 2 heaping tablespoons of salt in the palm of your right hand. Let a small amount of salt trickle into the glass while making the sign of the equal armed cross of the elements. Make this cross 3 times. Chant three times:

> ''Call me''

Set the glass on the altar and chant:

> ''Get in touch with me, please.''

They should contact you by the time the water has evaporated from the glass.
