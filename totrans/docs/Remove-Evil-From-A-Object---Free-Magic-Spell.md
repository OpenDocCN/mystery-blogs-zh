A spell to remove evil/negative energy/spirits from a object.

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   1 white candle

*   salt

*   object

*   pentacle

*   black cloth

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   1 white candle

*   salt

*   object

*   pentacle

*   black cloth

Take your salt and draw a circle around your object, make sure the circle is as thick and perfect as you can make it.

Now chant the following three or more times:

''Positive energy, stay with me

Evil cast away.
Leave you don't belong here

I demand all evil to flee.

Leave, leave. Hear my shout.

This is my will
