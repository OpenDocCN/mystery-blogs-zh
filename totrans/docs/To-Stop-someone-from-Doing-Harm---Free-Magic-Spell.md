# To Stop someone from Doing Harm

A spell to destroy a person's ability to do harm.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'To Stop someone from Doing Harm'

**You will need the following items for this spell:****You will need the following items for this spell:**Make wax models of the agressor's weapons (guns, knives, words, etc.)

Mold and shape these models so that they look as close to the actual objects themselves.

When finished, sit before a fire and take each model in your hands. Rub it slowly in your hands, disfiguring it. Say:

You are putty in my hands. You are powerless.

Repeat untill you feel the time is right. Then throw the ruined model into the fire and say:

You melt!

Visualize your enemy's power melting in the fire. Repeat with each model.
