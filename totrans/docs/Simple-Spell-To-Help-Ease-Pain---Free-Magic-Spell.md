# Simple Spell To Help Ease Pain

This spell with help ease and lessen pain.
Be it bones, or muscles, or guts.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Simple Spell To Help Ease Pain'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   Paper

*   Dark red, black, or skin color pencil. (Whatever color YOUR skin might be)

*   1 piece of quarts.

*   Matches

*   Paper

*   Dark red, black, or skin color pencil. (Whatever color YOUR skin might be)

First you need to locate exactly where the pain is.
Once you have done that, draw that part of your body, with out the hurt. (If it's visible)
Then write on the drawing with the black pen, what the pain feels like.

Once you are done with that, hold the paper to your heart and say the following three times.

''Pain in my (Say where it hurts)
I do not wish to suffer.
Disappear pain, or lessen.
Whatever you are that it hurting me so
You are not welcome here
So please just go!
Gods and Goddesses please help with my plea
So mote it be, so mote it be, so mote it be.''

Once you are done, fold the paper three times in any way.
Then use the dark red pen to draw a heart on the paper.
After this, burn the paper with one match.

The pain should go away or lessen within the next 3 to 33 minutes.
