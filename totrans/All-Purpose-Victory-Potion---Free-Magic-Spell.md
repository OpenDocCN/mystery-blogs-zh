*   3 cherries

*   1 blackberry leaf

To use:

Apply the potion to your skin. (Be sure to check with your doctor first to see if you're allergic to anything) This potion can even be used for things that that you didn't make the potion for. After all, this is all purpose, because the key ingredient is desire.
