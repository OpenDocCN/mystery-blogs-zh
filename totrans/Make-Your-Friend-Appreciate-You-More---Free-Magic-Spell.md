# Make Your Friend Appreciate You More

Makes a friend who has been taking you for granted appreciate you more. Does not happen immediately, comes through peace and understanding.

 ## Casting Instructions for 'Make Your Friend Appreciate You More'

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   1 object your friend ALWAYS carries with them

**You will need the following items for this spell:**

*   1 object your friend ALWAYS carries with them

When your friend isn't around, hold the object in the palm of your hand and chant:

''You are my weapon,
Yet you belong to him/her.
I am your master,
Yet your are his/her beck and call.
You have the power, I have the power.
Make him/her appreciate me a little bit more.''

Hopefully, as long as they carry/wear this item, they will not take you for granted anymore.
