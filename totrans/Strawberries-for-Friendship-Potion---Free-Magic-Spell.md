A simple and yummy potion for bringing friends to you.

*   Strawberries

*   Pitcher

*   Water

*   Lemon juice

*   Mint leaf (optional)

 1\. A day ahead of when you want to cast the spell, slice the strawberries and add them to the pitcher with some cold water. Let it sit in the fridge for about 24 hours, or earlier if you need to do the spell quickly.

2\. Pour a cup of the strawberry water. Recite the following, adding a drop of lemon juice at the start of every line.

3\. Stir clockwise and garnish with a mint leaf, if you so desire.
